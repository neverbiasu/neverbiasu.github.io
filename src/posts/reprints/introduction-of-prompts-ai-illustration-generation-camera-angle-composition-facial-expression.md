# Introduction of prompts in AI illustration generation

This guide covers prompts for AI illustration generation, focusing on camera angles, composition, and facial expressions to enhance your creative process.

## Camera angle and composition prompts

### Camera angle

| Prompt | Description | Image |
|--------|-------------|-------|
| `from the front`, `front view` | Shooting from the front. | ![Front view](https://neverbiasu.github.io/assets/images/reprints/digitalcreativeai/prompt-samples-from_the_front.jpg) |
| `from above`, `high angle` | Shooting from above. | ![High angle](https://neverbiasu.github.io/assets/images/reprints/digitalcreativeai/prompt-samples-from_above.jpg) |
| `from below`, `low angle` | Shooting from below. | ![Low angle](https://neverbiasu.github.io/assets/images/reprints/digitalcreativeai/prompt-samples-from_below.jpg) |
| `side view`, `side angle` | Shooting from the side. | ![Side view](https://neverbiasu.github.io/assets/images/reprints/digitalcreativeai/prompt-samples-side_view.jpg) |
| `back shot`, `back view` | Shooting from behind. Use `looking back` for a turned-around pose. | ![Back view](https://neverbiasu.github.io/assets/images/reprints/digitalcreativeai/prompt-samples-back_shot.jpg) |
| `overhead shot`, `bird's-eye view` | Shooting overhead, often for lying subjects. | ![Bird's-eye view](https://neverbiasu.github.io/assets/images/reprints/digitalcreativeai/prompt-samples-overhead_shot.jpg) |
| `pov` | First-person, subjective viewpoint. Combine with `pov hand` for hand perspective. | ![POV](https://neverbiasu.github.io/assets/images/reprints/digitalcreativeai/prompt-samples_pov.jpg)|
| `dutch angle` | Tilted shooting for eye-catching compositions. | ![Dutch angle](https://neverbiasu.github.io/assets/images/reprints/digitalcreativeai/prompt-samples-dutch_angle.jpg) |
