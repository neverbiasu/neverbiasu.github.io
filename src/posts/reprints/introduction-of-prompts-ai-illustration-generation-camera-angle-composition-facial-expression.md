## Camera angle and composition prompts

These are important prompts in illustration generation. The better you are able to use these prompts, the easier it will be to produce the composition you are aiming for.

Tilted Shooting

\*\*Prompt：\*\*dutch angle📋 Generates a composition with the camera tilted. This angle is more eye-catching than a normal composition.

### Framing height

![Image 12: sample of full body](https://www.digitalcreativeai.net/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fdca.data-hub-center.com%2Fcontent%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F12%2Fprompt-samples-full_body.jpg&w=1920&q=85)

Full-length

\*\*Prompt：\*\*full body📋full shot📋 Use when you want to capture the whole body. The full-body composition tends to be broken by the small size of the face, so use ADetailer or inpaint to correct it.

![Image 13: sample of medium shot](https://www.digitalcreativeai.net/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fdca.data-hub-center.com%2Fcontent%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F12%2Fprompt-samples-medium_shot.jpg&w=1920&q=85)

Crotch to top

\*\*Prompt：\*\*medium shot📋cowboy shot📋 It is used when you want to capture a person from the crotch up. If the aspect ratio is similar to the sample, you may want to look up to capture the person.

![Image 14: sample of medium close-shot](https://www.digitalcreativeai.net/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fdca.data-hub-center.com%2Fcontent%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F12%2Fprompt-samples-medium_close_shot.jpg&w=1920&q=85)

Abdomen to top

\*\*Prompt：\*\*medium close shot📋 It is a subtle height, but in some cases it can be a medium shot or a close shot.

![Image 15: sample of close shot](https://www.digitalcreativeai.net/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fdca.data-hub-center.com%2Fcontent%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F12%2Fprompt-samples-close_shot.jpg&w=1920&q=85)

Chest to top

\*\*Prompt：\*\*close shot📋close-up📋upper body📋portrait📋bust shot📋

![Image 16: sample of wide close-up](https://www.digitalcreativeai.net/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fdca.data-hub-center.com%2Fcontent%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F12%2Fprompt-samples-wide_close-up.jpg&w=1920&q=85)

Shoulders to top

\*\*Prompt：\*\*wide close-up📋 This prompt may not generate well depending on the aspect ratio.

![Image 17: sample of extreme close-up](https://www.digitalcreativeai.net/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fdca.data-hub-center.com%2Fcontent%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F12%2Fprompt-samples-extreme_close-up.jpg&w=1920&q=85)

Neck to top

\*\*Prompt：\*\*extreme close-up📋 In some cases, it is quite close.

![Image 18: sample of extreme close-up](https://www.digitalcreativeai.net/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fdca.data-hub-center.com%2Fcontent%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F12%2Fprompt-samples-lower_body.jpg&w=1920&q=85)

Lower half of body

\*\*Prompt：\*\*lower body📋 It is not often used, but if you are creating a story, there may come a time when you will use it.

## Facial Expression Prompts

![Image 19: Plutchik-wheel](https://www.digitalcreativeai.net/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fdca.data-hub-center.com%2Fcontent%2Fuploads%2F2024%2F12%2Fplutchik-wheel_en.png&w=1920&q=85)

Plutchik-wheel

This is a prompt for expressing facial expressions. This one is also a major factor in the quality of generation. It is roughly divided by Robert Plutchick’s [🔗8 primary emotions](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Emotion_classification#Plutchik's_wheel_of_emotions).
