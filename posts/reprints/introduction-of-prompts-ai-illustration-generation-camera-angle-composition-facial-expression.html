Introduction of prompts in AI illustration generation

This guide covers prompts for AI illustration generation, focusing on camera angles, composition, and facial expressions to enhance your creative process.

Camera angle and composition prompts

Camera angle

PromptDescriptionImage
from the front, front viewShooting from the front.
from above, high angleShooting from above.
from below, low angleShooting from below.
side view, side angleShooting from the side.
back shot, back viewShooting from behind. Use looking back for a turned-around pose.
overhead shot, bird's-eye viewShooting overhead, often for lying subjects.
povFirst-person, subjective viewpoint. Combine with pov hand for hand perspective.
dutch angleTilted shooting for eye-catching compositions.

Framing height

PromptDescriptionImage
full bodyCaptures the entire body.
half body, upper bodyCaptures from the waist up.
bust shot, close-upCaptures from the chest up.
head shot, portraitCaptures the face and shoulders.
extreme close-upFocuses on a specific part, such as the eyes.

Facial Expression Prompts

This is a prompt for expressing facial expressions. This one is also a major factor in the quality of generation. It is roughly divided by Robert Plutchick’s 🔗8 primary emotions.

PromptDescriptionImage
smileA normal smile with the corners of the mouth lifted. Sometimes the mouth opens if the weights are lifted.
laughLaugh with a wide mouth.
giggling, chucklingSmile with a wide open mouth. The eyes will also smile.
laughing out loudThe so-called "LOL"; we use it without abbreviation because lol did not work for us.
grinGrin with bared teeth.
smirk, smugUse this when you want to make a smug face.
joyful, delightedExpressions of joy are expressed not only with the face but also with gestures.
cheerfulUse this when you want to generate a cheerful feeling.
pleasedA pleasant expression.
amusedGenerates an amused expression.
interested, curiousGenerates an expression of curiosity or interest.
attractedGenerates an expression of attraction.
excitedGenerates an excited expression, often involving the whole body.

arguingGenerates an expression of arguing, often involving multiple people.
poutyGenerates a sulking or pouty expression.
jealousGenerates a jealous expression.

grumpyGenerates a grumpy expression.
contemptuousGenerates a contemptuous expression.
not impressedGenerates an indifferent expression.
exhaustedGenerates an exhausted expression.

Conclusion

How was it? Prompts are the most important parameter for AI generation. We have introduced the composition and facial expression prompts in this article, but it would take a long time to cover all of them, so we will introduce them in a separate article.
